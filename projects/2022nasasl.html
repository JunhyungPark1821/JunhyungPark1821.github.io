2022 NASA Student Launch Competition
(Aug 2021 - May 2022)

Click to find out more about NASA Student Launch Competition

Mission

"Teams shall design a payload capable of autonomously locating the launch vehicle upon landing by identifying the launch vehicle’s grid position on an aerial image of the launch site without the use of a global positioning system (GPS). The method(s)/design(s) utilized to complete the payload mission will be at the teams’ discretion and will be permitted so long as the designs are deemed safe, obey FAA and legal requirements, and adhere to the intent of the challenge." ~ From 2022 NASA Student Launch Handbook

Development

  • Send three-star general’s flight cap to near-space!
  • Research Long Range (LoRa) Radio Transmission System's capability to transfer flight data real time for potentially making weather balloon research cheaper.
